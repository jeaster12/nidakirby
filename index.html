About Me

I've been doing hair for over 30 years, 10 of which have been in Holly Springs. I am very fortunate to work in an industry where we help people feel great about themselves. Hairstyles have changed a lot since the 80s but the basic techniques remain the same.
I have extremely curly hair and love to teach others how to embrace their curls.

I've been married 28 years and have three beautiful children well on their way to discovering all they can be. We moved to Holly Springs in 1999 from Pittsburgh and just love it here!
